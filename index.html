Coming to America

Coming to America follows Prince Akeem Joffer of Zamunda, who travels to America with his loyal friend Semmi to find a wife who will love him for who he is, not his royal status. Disguised as a poor man, Akeem lands in Queens, New York, and takes a job at a fast food restaurant owned by Lisa McDowell. As he falls in love with Lisa, he struggles to maintain his secret while dealing with his overbearing father, King Jaffe, who insists Akeem return home and fulfill his royal duties. Akeem and Semmi navigate a series of comedic situations, including misunderstandings and cultural clashes. Ultimately, Akeem proves that love, not wealth, is the key to happiness, leading to a joyful conclusion with Lisa.
